About Us

HIRED is a website that provides data and insights related to job postings and employment trends. Our mission is to help job seekers from UMD make informed decisions by providing easy-to-use tools for exploring job market data in Metropolitan areas.

HIRED uses a variety of data sources to provide accurate and up-to-date information about job openings, employment trends, job posting sources, location, and more. Our team is constantly updating and improving our data to ensure that we provide the best possible insights to our users.

If you have any feedback or suggestions for how we can improve HIRED, please don't hesitate to contact us at hired@gmail.com
